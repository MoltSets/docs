## Before You Begin
 
The examples in this guide are designed for use with the MoltSets MCP (Model Context Protocol) server connected to Claude or another AI agent. The "Type in Claude" prompts will only work once your AI tool is connected to MoltSets — without it, Claude has no access to MoltSets tools or your account data.
 
If you haven't set that up yet, [connect your AI agent to the MoltSets MCP](/moltsets-mcp) before proceeding.
 
---

Pick your starting point below. Each table shows what input you need, what you'll get back, an example prompt to use in Claude, and the tool name.

## Search

Search MoltSets's company and contact database by name, domain, industry, employee count, or revenue range. Use these endpoints to build B2B prospect lists, identify target accounts, and find contacts that match your ideal customer profile.

| I have… | I want… | Type in Claude… | Tool |
|---|---|---|---|
| Company name | Matching company profiles (name, domain, industry, size, revenue) | Find company profiles matching Acme | `search_company_profiles` |
| Domain | Exact company record | Look up the company at acme.com | `search_company_profiles` |
| Industry + employee size or revenue range | Filtered list of companies | Find information technology companies with 51-200 employees | `search_company_profiles` |
| Company name or domain | All contacts at that company | Find contacts at acme.com | `search_business_profiles` |
| Company name + industry filter | Contacts filtered by industry | Find information technology contacts at acme.com | `search_business_profiles` |
| Company name + employee size | Contacts at companies of a specific size | Find contacts at companies with 51-200 employees named Acme | `search_business_profiles` |
| Revenue range | Contacts at companies within a revenue band | Find contacts at companies with revenue above $50M | `search_business_profiles` |
| Industry + employee size | Contacts matching an ICP profile | Find contacts at information technology companies with 201-500 employees | `search_business_profiles` |

## Get Verified Emails

Resolve LinkedIn profile URLs, company domains, email addresses, and IP addresses into verified business and personal email addresses. Supports single lookups, batch enrichment, and multi-step chains for building outbound contact lists with high deliverability.

| I have… | I want… | Type in Claude… | Tool |
|---|---|---|---|
| LinkedIn URL | Best available email (business if found, falls back to personal) | Find the best email for linkedin.com/in/username | `enrich_email` |
| LinkedIn URL | Business email only | Find the business email for linkedin.com/in/username | `enrich_business_email` |
| LinkedIn URL | Personal email only | Find the personal email for linkedin.com/in/username | `enrich_personal_email` |
| LinkedIn URL | Highest-confidence personal email | Find the best personal email for linkedin.com/in/username | `linkedin_to_best_personal_email` |
| Company name or domain | Best available email for each employee | Search for employees at acme.com and get their best email address | `search_business_profiles` → `enrich_email` |
| Company name or domain | Business emails for each employee | Search for employees at acme.com and get their business email addresses | `search_business_profiles` → `enrich_business_email` |
| Company name or domain | Personal emails for each employee | Search for employees at acme.com and get their personal email addresses | `search_business_profiles` → `enrich_personal_email` |
| IP address | Emails for employees at the company behind that IP | Find employees at the company behind IP 203.0.113.42 and get their email addresses | `ip_to_company` → `search_business_profiles` → `enrich_email` |
| Email address | Personal email as a backup or alternative | Find the LinkedIn profile for john.smith@example.com then get their personal email | `hem_to_linkedin` → `enrich_personal_email` |
| LinkedIn URL | Business email, falling back to personal if not found | Try to find the business email for linkedin.com/in/username, fall back to personal if nothing is found | `enrich_business_email` → `enrich_personal_email` |

## Get Carrier-Verified Mobile Phones

Look up carrier-verified mobile phone numbers from LinkedIn profiles, email addresses, company domains, and IP addresses. Every number returned is a real mobile device — no landlines or VoIP. Supports single lookups and batches of up to 100 profiles at once, as well as multi-step chains for building call and SMS lists directly from a company name or IP address.

| I have… | I want… | Type in Claude… | Tool |
|---|---|---|---|
| LinkedIn URL | Mobile phone number | Find the mobile phone number for linkedin.com/in/username | `enrich_phone` |
| List of LinkedIn URLs (up to 100) | Mobile phone numbers for each profile | Find mobile phone numbers for these LinkedIn profiles: [list] | `enrich_phone` |
| Company name or domain | Mobile phone numbers for each employee | Search for employees at acme.com and get their mobile phone numbers | `search_business_profiles` → `enrich_phone` |
| Company name + seniority or role filter | Mobile phones for decision-makers at a company | Search for senior contacts at acme.com and get their mobile phone numbers | `search_business_profiles` → `enrich_phone` |
| Email address | Mobile phone number | Find the LinkedIn profile for john.smith@example.com then get their mobile phone number | `hem_to_linkedin` → `enrich_phone` |
| IP address | Mobile phones for employees at the company behind that IP | Find employees at the company behind IP 203.0.113.42 and get their mobile phone numbers | `ip_to_company` → `search_business_profiles` → `enrich_phone` |
| LinkedIn URL | Email address and mobile phone | Get the email address and mobile phone number for linkedin.com/in/username | `enrich_email` + `enrich_phone` |

## Get Ad Audience

Convert known contact identifiers — LinkedIn URLs and email addresses — into hashed emails (SHA-256 and MD5) and mobile advertising IDs (MAIDs) for privacy-safe audience matching. Use these endpoints to activate your contact lists across Meta, Google, LinkedIn Ads, TikTok, and any programmatic platform that supports customer match or device-level targeting.

| I have… | I want… | Type in Claude… | Tool |
|---|---|---|---|
| Business email | Personal hashed emails (SHA-256) for audience matching | Get personal hashed emails for john.smith@example.com | `business_email_to_personal_hem` |
| LinkedIn URL | All known hashed emails (MD5 + SHA-256, business + personal) | Get all hashed emails for linkedin.com/in/username | `linkedin_to_hashed_emails` |
| Email | Mobile advertising IDs (MAIDs) | Get mobile ad IDs for john.smith@example.com | `hem_to_maid` |

## Get Company

Identify the company behind any IPv4 address and use that signal to trigger downstream enrichment — finding contacts, pulling a full company profile, or surfacing decision-makers at the organisation. Best suited for B2B use cases; consumer ISP addresses will typically not resolve to a named company.

| I have… | I want… | Type in Claude… | Tool |
|---|---|---|---|
| IP address | Company associated with that IP | What company is behind IP 203.0.113.42 | `ip_to_company` |
| IP address | Contacts at the company behind that IP | Find contacts at the company behind IP 203.0.113.42 | `ip_to_company` → `search_business_profiles` |
| IP address | Full company profile for the organisation behind that IP | Look up the full company profile for the business at IP 203.0.113.42 | `ip_to_company` → `search_company_profiles` |
| IP address | Decision-makers at the company behind that IP | Find senior contacts at the company behind IP 203.0.113.42 | `ip_to_company` → `search_business_profiles` |

## Common Workflows

Some tasks require chaining two tools together. These are the most useful combinations.

### Business email to mobile phone

`hem_to_linkedin` → `enrich_phone`

You have a business email but no LinkedIn URL. Resolve it to a LinkedIn slug first, then use that to get a mobile phone number.

"Find the LinkedIn profile for john.smith@example.com, then get their mobile phone number."

---

### LinkedIn list to mobile ad audience

`linkedin_to_hashed_emails` → `hem_to_maid`

You have a list of LinkedIn profiles and want to push them into a mobile ad platform. Get all known HEMs for each profile, then resolve those to MAIDs.

"Get all hashed emails for linkedin.com/in/username, then resolve them to mobile ad IDs."

---

### CRM email list to ad retargeting audience

`business_email_to_personal_hem` → `hem_to_maid`

You have a CRM list of business emails and want to build a retargeting audience. Convert each business email to a personal HEM, then resolve to MAIDs.

"Convert john.smith@example.com to a personal hashed email, then resolve it to mobile ad IDs."
