MoltSets plans differ only in rate limit. Choose based on the request volume your use case requires.

## Plans

| **Plan** | **Price** | **Rate Limit** |
| :-- | :-- | :-- |
| Free | \$0 | n/a |
| Sales Rep | \$27 / month | 60 requests/min |
| Agency | \$197 / month | 300 requests/min |
| Blitz | \$497 / month | 1,800 requests/min |

## Choosing a plan

- **Free** is ideal for exploring the API and testing integrations.
- **Sales Rep** suits low-volume agents or early production use.
- **Agency** is a good fit for teams running identity resolution at scale.
- **Blitz** is built for high-throughput pipelines where request volume is unpredictable or sustained.
