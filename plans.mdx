MoltSets plans differ only in rate limit. Choose based on the request volume your use case requires.

## Plans

| **Plan** | **Lifetime Tokens** | **Price** | **Rate Limit** |
| :-- | :-- | :-- | :-- |
| Free | 100 | \$0 | n/a |
| Sales Rep | Unlimited | \$27 / month | 60 requests/min |
| Agency | Unlimited | \$197 / month | 300 requests/min |
| Blitz | Unlimited | \$497 / month | 1,800 requests/min |

## Choosing a plan

The right plan depends on how fast you need results. Higher rate limits mean faster processing — the difference between waiting an hour and wrapping up in minutes.

| **Plan** | **Ideal for** | **5,000 records** |
| :-- | :-- | :-- |
| Free | Exploring the API and testing integrations | n/a |
| Sales Rep | Low-volume agents or early production use | ~83 min |
| Agency | Teams running identity resolution at scale | ~17 min |
| Blitz | High-throughput pipelines with sustained or unpredictable volume | ~3 min |
